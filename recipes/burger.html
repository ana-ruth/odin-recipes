Burger Recipe

Description

Today we will learn how to make a tasty beef burger.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Beef patty
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mustard

Steps:

  1. Grill beef patty until meat is no longer pink in the center.
  2. Add mustard to the bread.
  3. On top of the patty place one cheese slice.
  4. Assemble burger with all ingredients.
