Pizza Recipe

Description

Today we will learn how to make a meat lovers pizza.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Italian Sausage
  • Bacon

Steps:

  1. Preheat the Oven.
  2. Prepare the Crust.
  3. Cook the sausage.
  4. Prepare the Pan.
  5. Shape the Dough.
  6. Add the toppings.
  7. Bake the pizza.
  8. Enjoy!
