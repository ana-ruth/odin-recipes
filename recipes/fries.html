Fries Recipe

Description

Today we will learn how to make french fries.

Ingredients:

  • Starchy Potatoes
  • Vinegar
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Cut fries.
  2. Keep fries in water to prevent browning.
  3. Rinse.
  4. Cook in vinegar water.
  5. Drain and Dry.
  6. Fry.
  7. Let them cool.
  8. Fry them again.
  9. Add seasoning.
